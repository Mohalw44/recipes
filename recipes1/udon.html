Udon

Ingredients You’ll Need For This Recipe

This Black Pepper Stir Fried Udon is the perfect SOS dinner!

  • udon noodles
  • a bag of stir fry vegetables
  • dark soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic

Now let’s get cooking!

How To Make It

  1. Sauce:Shake up all the sauce ingredients in a jar.
  2. Noodles:Heat a nonstick skillet over medium high heat
  3. Veggies:Add the veggies and pour a little more sauce over everything
  4. Finish:Remove from heat and stir in some freshly grated garlic and A TON of freshly ground black pepper.
