Ingredients You’ll Need For This Recipe

This Spicy Peanut Soba Noodle Salad

  • peanut butter
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • juice of 1 lime
  • chicken breast
  • clove garlic

Now let’s get cooking!

How To Make It

  1. Spicy Peanut Sauce: Puree sauce ingredients in a food processor or blender.
  2. Chicken:Cook the chicken in a skillet over medium high heat
  3. Vegetables:Chop the red pepper, cabbage, and cilantro to your desired size
  4. Noodles:Cook the noodles according to package directions (usually just boiling for a few minutes).
  5. Assemble: Toss everything together with enough dressing to generously coat everything. Serve hot or cold!
